How it works

From sign-up to your first encrypted chat

Four steps, in order — each one unlocks the next.

01

Create your account

Pick a username and password, add a profile photo if you like, and you're in — no email required.

02

Share your first post

Upload a photo, write a caption, tag a location. It shows up in your followers' feed right away.

03

Send a chat request

Find someone in People, send a request. Once they accept, a private encryption key is created just for that conversation.

04

Verify and talk freely

Tap the lock icon to compare your safety number, then message, react, and share photos — encrypted the whole way.
